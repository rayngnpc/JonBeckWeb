A Photographer's Philosophy

"I am drawn to the diversity of the world – from Australia's red soil centre, the character in peoples faces and the unique textures of our building heritage. My combined passion for the outdoors, people and their environment enables me to create iconic images from Australia's diverse landscape, New Zealand and the world."

"I believe photographs have to provoke an emotion, represent uniqueness and tell a story – but with a difference."

Professional Excellence

Jon Beck is an international award-winning photographer with over 15 years of professional experience. His approach to photography is one of no compromise – from the initial capture to the final print and frame, only the finest quality materials are used.

Jon combines precise technical skills with an approachable and personable demeanor to help clients achieve their vision. His pictures are not mere souvenirs – they are images that help us to 'see' and understand landscapes and people as art.

Light literally exudes from Jon's pictures like few others, and this luminescent enlightenment startles us with its clarity and perception. His work decorates the walls of homes, offices, and commercial spaces around Australia and the world.

Inspiration & Location

Living in Queensland, Jon is surrounded by beautiful beaches, the unique characters of its people, and ideal weather – all of which serve as inspiration for his stunning images. He constantly strives to capture spectacular light and mood within his photographs, while maintaining a fresh, contemporary perspective.

From Australia's red soil centre to the character in people's faces and the unique textures of building heritage, Jon's diverse portfolio showcases his ability to find beauty and meaning in the world around us.
